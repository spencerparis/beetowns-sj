About Us

Bee towns is a project aimed at helping bees thrive all around the county. Bees are a vital part of the ecosystem and without they most of the amazing cornish environment wouldnt exist. Therefore, we should aim to help them as much as we can and we are giving you a way to do that. Look on our map to find the cell you live or work inside of, make a bee stop at your place of work or residence and then upload a picture to our website to claim your cell! This helps the bees get around the county and puts you and your stop on our website to show that you are doing your part.
